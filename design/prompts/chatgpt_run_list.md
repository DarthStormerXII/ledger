## Iteration tips if a prompt misfires

- **White background** → restate at the front: *"BACKGROUND: deep navy black #0A0E1A. DO NOT use white. The frame is dark edge-to-edge."*
- **Dashed bottom edge gone** → restate: *"The bottom edge between the two lower circles is a DASHED line. It must be present and dashed, not solid, not omitted."*
- **3D / glow crept in** → append: *"Pure flat vector. Zero depth, zero glow, zero bloom. Like a logo printed in ink on paper."*
- **Wrong layout** → tell it the exact pixel intent: *"Mark on the left, occupying leftmost 30% of the canvas. Wordmark on the right, occupying remaining 70%. Vertically centered."*
